About us

Led by structured note experience

Structured Note Insight is built around a simple idea: advisors and investors should be able to quickly see how a note works, what it costs, and what today’s markets mean for expected outcomes.

Steve Boyle has worked across capital markets, structured products, and advisor support, helping financial professionals translate complex terms into practical guidance for clients. His background spans product design, due diligence, and portfolio construction, with a focus on communication that stands up in both client meetings and compliance reviews.

Structured Note Insight grew out of that experience: a desire to take the information that already exists in pricing supplements, investor disclosures, and market data, and present it in a way that makes it easier to compare notes, document recommendations, and monitor holdings over time.

The platform is not a recommendation engine and does not replace firm-approved research. Instead, it aims to be a clear lens into what a note is actually doing and how that lines up with the expectations set when it was purchased.
